    Danielle Kirlin, former actress, is widely known as the wife of actor and producer Ryan McPartlin.

    A Quincy, Illinois native, Danielle Kirlin transitioned from the world of acting to entrepreneurship, marking her as a woman of diverse talents.

    Currently residing in Santa Clarita, California, Kirlin holds the esteemed position of Chief Executive Officer (CEO) & Founder at Plate Therapy, showcasing her leadership and business acumen.

    Before her venture into entrepreneurship, Kirlin made her mark in the entertainment industry as an actress. Her notable roles include portraying Zoe in “Centipede!” (2004) and taking on the character of a Flight Attendant in “Felicity” (2002). Though her acting days may be in the rearview mirror, her journey into the business world adds another layer to her fascinating story.

    The love story between Danielle Kirlin and Ryan McPartlin began in 1995 at the University of Illinois during a college ski trip. Their connection blossomed, leading to a deep and enduring romance.

    After over five years of dating, the couple sealed their commitment in a beautiful ceremony on October 26, 2002.

    Through their union, Danielle Kirlin and Ryan McPartlin share two children; Wyatt Duke McPartlin and Dylan James McPartlin.

    The McPartlin family currently calls Santa Clarita, California, their home, where they navigate the intricacies of both family life and the entertainment industry.
