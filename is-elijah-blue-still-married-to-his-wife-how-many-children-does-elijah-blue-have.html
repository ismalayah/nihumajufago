    About Elijah Blue Allman

    Elijah Blue Allman, also known as P. Exeter Blue, born in 1976, carries a musical legacy as the son of iconic singer Cher and Gregg Allman of the Allman Brothers Band.

    His musical journey led him to front the industrial metal band Deadsy, where he lends his vocals, lyrics, and guitar skills. Deadsy’s releases have garnered a dedicated fanbase within the genre.

    However, Allman’s path has been marked by personal struggles. He has faced battles with substance abuse and Lyme disease, even seeking treatment in Germany.

    In 2023, Cher made headlines by filing for conservatorship over Elijah’s estate, expressing concerns about his alleged financial mismanagement due to ongoing challenges.

    Despite these hurdles, Elijah Blue Allman stands as a notable figure in the music world, carving his unique niche in rock music.
